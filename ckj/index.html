about us

Originators of Fine Jewelers Since 1953
Mastering the art of jewelery making since 1953, Mr. Chris Kourkoulakos came to Washington, DC in 1964 from Greece. Nine years after serving the DC area in the industry, Mr. Kourkoulakos decided to open his own store in the heart of the capital.


Over thirty five years later, Chris Kourkoulakos Jewelers, is still reputably one of the best jewelers in the area, setting the standard in customized jewelry service.
