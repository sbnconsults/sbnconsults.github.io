About US

Having been such an integral part of the community and serving the residents of Georgetown for over 27 years, the former Georgetown Dinette has reopened as GTown Bites! Our mission is to bring a diverse and health conscious new menu with delicious sliders, gyros, salads, fresh juices, and more to Georgetown. We are conveniently located on the corner of O Street and Wisconsin Avenue in the heart of DC's historic Georgetown neighborhood. The new owners, Nasser and Josh, have already become neighborhood favorites with their great prices, friendly staff, family atmosphere, and amazing food! GTown Bites has something for everyone; our menu provides healthy vegetarian and vegan options as well!

"This place is worth a visit, definitely a new gem in Georgetown."

With such a rich history to preserve, it was no easy task reopening the diner. Nasser and Josh have not only brought back the establishment in all its glory, they have surpassed expectations with great food and a welcoming atmosphere. GTown Bites has what you would expect from a regular diner, but brings a unique twist to our burgers made with flavorful spices and special sauces that can only be found here! Catering is also available, call today to find out details about our catering options. Seating is available both inside the diner and outside in our new covered garden patio, (which also grows fresh herbs that are used in the food and juices).
